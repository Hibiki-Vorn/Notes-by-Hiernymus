## 串联和并联

- parallel 并联
	- $V = Constant$
	- $I$ is inversely proportional to $R$
	- $T_{total}=\sum^{n}_{j=1}I_j$
- serirs 串联
	- $I = Constant$ 
	- $V$ is directly proportional to $R$
	- $R_{total}=\sum^{n}_{j=1} R_{j}$
	- $1/R_{total}=\sum^{n}_{j=1}{(1/R_j)}$
		1. $R_{total}<min(R)$
		2. if $R_{1}=R_{2}=...=R_{n}$ then $R_{total}=\frac{R}{n}$
