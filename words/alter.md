1. **Definition**  
	- v. 改变；修改  
	- n. 改动；变更  

2. **Example Sentence**  
	- 1. The tailor altered the dress to fit perfectly.  
		裁缝修改了连衣裙使其更合身。  
	- 2. We had to alter our plans due to the bad weather.  
		由于天气不好，我们不得不改变计划。  
	- 3. The document cannot be altered once it's been signed.  
		文件一旦签署就不能更改。  
	- 4. The landscape has been altered dramatically by the new construction.  
		新建工程使景观发生了巨大变化。  

3. **Synonym**  
	- 1. modify 修改  
	- 2. adjust 调整  
	- 3. change 改变  
	- 4. amend 修正  
	- 5. revise 修订  

4. **Antonym**  
	- 1. maintain 保持  
	- 2. preserve 保留  
	- 3. keep 保持  
	- 4. fix 固定  
	- 5. stabilize 稳定
