1. **Definition**
    
    - v. 咀嚼；嚼碎；反复思考
    - n. 咀嚼的动作；咀嚼物
2. **Example Sentence**
    
    - 1. She chewed her food slowly, savoring each bite.  
            她慢慢地咀嚼食物，细细品味每一口。
    - 2. The dog chewed on a toy for hours.  
            那只狗啃了一个玩具好几个小时。
    - 3. He had to chew over the idea before making a decision.  
            他在做决定之前需要反复思考这个想法。
    - 4. After chewing the gum for a while, it lost its flavor.  
            嚼了那块口香糖一会儿后，它的味道就没了。
3. **Synonym**
    
    - 1. nibble 啃咬
    - 2. munch 大口咀嚼
    - 3. grind 磨碎
    - 4. bite 咬
    - 5. gnaw 咬
4. **Antonym**
    
    - 1. swallow 吞咽
    - 2. spit 吐
    - 3. gulp 狼吞虎咽
    - 4. inhale 吸入
    - 5. disregard 忽略
